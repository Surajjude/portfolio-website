Dice Roll — Two Player Dice Game

A web-based two-player dice game powered by a simple page-refreshing technique. Refresh the window to roll both dice with random faces — the player who lands a 6 wins. If both show the same face, it's a draw!

🎲 How It Works

Refresh the page to roll both dice simultaneously. Each die shows a random face (1–6). The player who gets a 6 wins — if both match, it's a draw!
